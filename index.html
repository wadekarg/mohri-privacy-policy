Privacy Policy for Mohri

Last updated: March 10, 2025

Overview

Mohri is a task management application for macOS that prioritizes your privacy. This privacy policy explains how we handle your data.

Data Storage

Mohri stores all your task data locally on your Mac. Your tasks, notes, comments, attachments, and all other information remain on your device and are not sent to any external servers by default.

iCloud Sync and Sharing (Optional)

If you choose to use the task sharing features, Mohri uses Apple's CloudKit to sync shared tasks between users. This data is:

  • Stored securely in your iCloud account
  • Protected by Apple's security and privacy policies
  • Only accessible to users you explicitly share tasks with
  • Encrypted during transmission and storage

Data Collection

Mohri does not collect, store, track, or share any personal information beyond what you choose to store in your tasks. Specifically:

  • We do not collect analytics or usage data
  • We do not track your activities
  • We do not sell or share your data with third parties
  • We do not use advertising or tracking technologies

Shared Tasks

When you share a task with another user:

  • Only the specific task information you choose to share is transmitted via iCloud
  • Recipients can only see tasks you explicitly share with them
  • You control who has access to your shared tasks
  • You can revoke sharing access at any time

Mentions and Collaboration

When you mention another user in a task or comment, that user will receive a notification (if enabled in their system settings). Mentions are only sent to users who already have access to the shared task.

File Attachments

Any files or images you attach to tasks are stored locally on your device. If you share a task with attachments, those attachments are securely transmitted via iCloud only to the users you've shared the task with.

Data Security

Mohri uses industry-standard security practices:

  • All data transmission uses Apple's secure CloudKit infrastructure
  • Local data is protected by macOS security features
  • The app is sandboxed and follows Apple's security guidelines

Your Rights

You have complete control over your data:

  • You can delete any task, comment, or attachment at any time
  • You can stop using sharing features and keep all data local
  • You can delete the app and all associated data from your device

Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page with an updated "Last updated" date. Continued use of Mohri after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

Children's Privacy

Mohri is not directed to children under the age of 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from children.
